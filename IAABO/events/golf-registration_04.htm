2004 Ref’s Invitational Four Person Scramble

GOLF TOURNAMENT
Sunday June 27, 2004 at 9:30 a.m.
Bar Harbor Golf Course, Trenton, Maine
$40 per golfer - $160 per team
click here to register on-line

Cost of the tournament includes greens fees, prizes and lunch. There will also be par three contests, longest and straightest drives for men and women.
Cart rentals are extra through the pro shop.

This tournament is open to all officials, coaches, AD’s, teachers, and family members and friends.
Foursomes who wish to play together may do so. Please specify all names on the registration form. Individuals may sign up and teams will be assigned. Golfers without handicaps, please note average 18 hole score.

Please make full payments for all golfers listed on registration form. Deadline for payments is June 1, 2004. Entry received after June 1, 2004 will be $45 per golfer and $180 per team. Make checks payable to:
