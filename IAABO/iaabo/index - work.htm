January 2018 Meeting

Board #111's January Meeting will be held at 10am on Jan 7th, 2018 at Bennett Hall on the University of Maine Campus. This is the last meeting of the season before for the Spring Board Meeting. If you still need points toward being a member in good standing or toward your ratings this will be the last chance to receive them.

We look forward to seeing you on the 7th!
