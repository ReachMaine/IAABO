Dear Maine IAABO members,

IAABO Board #111 is once again sponsoring a Fall Conference to prepare for the upcoming season. This event is a one-day seminar at Hampden Academy in the Performing Arts Center on SUNDAY, November 20th. Please use the front doors to Hampden Academy.

7:45-8:30am Conference Registration
8:50-9:50am MPA Clinic
9:10:00-12:00 Presenters
12:10-1:00 pm Lunch on site in the Hampden Academy cafeteria
1:15-3:30 Presenters
3:45- IAABO #111 Fall Business Meeting

For IAABO Board #111 members, the Fall Conference counts as four clinics for 12 of the 15 points needed to meet the ratings requirement. There will be a regional meeting held on a date other than the Refresher Test Review date that will count as three (3) points towards the required 15.

There will also be a December and January Clinic that can count towards the 15 points and if one already has 15 meeting points then the December and January clinics would each count as one half (.5) bonus point. The Refresher Test Review Session DOES NOT count for clinic points but meets the requirement of turning in the Refresher test.

Per the membership wishes of IAABO #111, the Fall Business Meeting will be held immediately after the Fall Conference.

Honigs will be on site at the fall conference from 8:00am-1:00pm for all your 2016-17officiating needs.

***Also, everyone is encouraged to bring a non-perishable food item to donate to local food cupboards.

The fee for this year's conference registration is $35 which includes coffee/water during the conference and lunch. Registration deadline is 11/14/16 (late fee registration fee is $40)
