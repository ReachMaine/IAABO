  11. The remaining agenda items were tabled until the March 8, 2014 meeting at 8:30 am in the conference room at Reeds Brook Middle School due to the fact that Dave Ames and Nick left at noon, Emil at 10:30 am leaving only eight members in attendance.
     

  12. Meeting adjourned shortly after noon on February 20, 2014
