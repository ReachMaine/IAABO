MINUTES
EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE MEETING
SUNDAY,  SEPTEMBER 14, 2014
HAMPDEN ACADEMY, HAMPDEN, ME

Present: Chris, Wayne, Gayle, Bill, Nick, Fred, John, Emil, Sheridan, Randy, & Shawn

Other Present: Dave Ames

  1. President Wadleigh welcomed everyone and called the meeting to order at 9:00 am

  2. The BOD reviewed the draft minutes of the Spring 2014 Business Meeting

  3. The BOD approved the Executive Committee Minutes for March 8, 2014. Motion by Chris and seconded by Nick Vote: (U) Motion passed

  4. The minutes of the Executive Committee of the Board of Directors for April 24, 2014 and June 16, 2014 were approved. Motion by Emil and seconded by Wayne Vote: (U) Motion passed

  5. Secretary’s Report: The Secretary thanked the committee for all their cards and emails during his recent illness. There are currently 117 active officials, 14 non-officiating officials for a total membership of 131 members. There are 11 female officials and 11 new officials have joined the board. 20 individuals currently have unpaid dues for 2014-2015. This will have an effect of the income side of the budget for 2014-2015. Income will be down about $1,500 compared to the budget presented at the Spring 2014 Business Meeting. Insurance for officials has been paid for 2014-2015 and the Officers and Directors Insurance for 2014-2015 has also been paid.
    A motion was made by Wayne and seconded by Chris to approve the Secretary’s report. Vote: (U) Motion passed

  6. Dates and Times of Future Meeting:

    • October 5th Orientation for new officials at 4:00 pm

    • October 19th Board of Directors at 3:00 pm

    • Sunday, November 16th Fall Conference at Husson University 8:00-4:00

    • Sunday, November 16th Fall Business Meeting at Husson University at 4:00 pm

    • Sunday, November 30th Exam Night at the University of Maine. Review at 6:00 pm & Written and Manual Exam at 7:00 pm

    • Sunday, December 14th Clinic at University of Maine at 2:00 pm

    • Sunday, January 11, 2015 Clinic at University of Maine at 10:00 am

    • Sunday, February 15, 2015 Board of Directors meeting at 9:00 am

      Dates for Refresher Review and Regional Clinics to be announced A motion by Bill and seconded by Gayle to accept the date and times for future meetings. Vote: (U) Motion passed

  7. Health Questionnaire was discussed by the BOD. A motion was made by Wayne and Seconded by Emil to eliminate the health questionnaire. Vote: (U) Motion passed

  8. President’s Report: Discussion of meeting with PVC reps Mr. Dow and Mr. Murphy. The discussion was about eliminating the school rating. The PVC voted at their September meeting to keep the school ratings. Nokomis High School in now part of our assignment system. Discussion of background checks which was discussed at the Council Meeting in March. A motion was made to invite Danielle Murphy to do a presentation of background checks to Board #111 at the Fall 2014 Business Meeting and to pay her mileage for the trip. Motion by Fred and seconded by Chris. Vote: 10-1-0 Motion passed

  9. Discussion of shared agreement with Board 18 and Board 111. There has been discussion between the two board presidents. A motion was made by Fred and seconded by Shawn to table the issue. Vote: 10-0-1 Motion passed

  10. Pre-season Games: The rate for pre-season varsity games will be the varsity rate with no travel which was voted on at the Council meeting in March. No action

  11. Some schools will be doing three person pre-season and officials will receive no fee in exchange for schools having three person assignments during the regular season. No action

  12. Mentoring Pilot Program: Committee is Fred, Sheridan, & Randy. Interpreter is important part of the program. Sheridan to take the lead on the program. The program is for officials in the 5 to 10 bracket. There will be 15 mentors and 15 mentees to start the program. There will be a meeting with Commissioner Webb in late October. A motion by Shawn and seconded by Wayne to accept the program. Vote: (U) Motion passed

  13. Assignment Discussion: Assignment review committee has met. No real concerns. PVC has concerns on travel pay. PVC informed that Assigner is not part of the Board of Directors under the new By-Laws. There was a concern about officials working with the same partner for the majority of their games

  14. Assignment Policies: Wayne presented a draft of the revisions to the assignment policies. The revisions were reviewed by the BOD and comments were taken.

  15. Interpreter’s Report: Orientation for new members for 2014 will be on October 5th at 4:00 pm at Reeds Brook Middle School in Room 164. Fall Conference November 16th at Husson University. There was discussion about the presentations for the conference. There will be 4 clinics at the conference. Clinics will be 1 ½ hours, plus there will be a refresher clinic, and one regional clinic. Level issues tabled until next meeting. Prospective class for 2015 has had interest from about 20 individuals.

  16. Discussion on convicted felon question on prospective application. A motion was made by Nick and seconded by Chris to include on the application. Vote: (U) Motion passed.

  17. A motion was made by Chris and seconded by Wayne to have an Executive Session to discuss a personnel issue. Time In: 11:30 am Time Out: 11:40 am No action taken

  18. Future agenda item(s): Executive Session to discuss a personnel issue and Discussion of Levels on the rating system

  19. A motion was made by Chris and seconded by Gayle to adjourn at 11:42 am. Vote: (U) Motion passed

    (Fred and Sheridan left at 11:02 am)

Respectfully submitted,
John A. Doe, Secretary
