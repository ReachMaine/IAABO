MINUTES
EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE MEETING
OCTOBER 19, 2014
HAMPDEN ACADEMY

Present: Chris, Bill, Fred, Nick, Gayle, Sheridan, Emil, John, & Randy
Absent: Wayne & Shawn
Other(s) Present: Dave Ames

  1. Meeting called to order at 3:00 pm by President Wadleigh

  2. A motion by Emil seconded by Chris to approve the minutes of the September 14, 2014 meeting. Vote (U) Motion passed

  3. Secretary’s Report: The secretary has not received any dues payments since the last meeting on September 14, 2014. There are currently 131 members of Board #111. The Region 10 report from Barry Fuller was discussed. There was discussion on the online refresher exam. There was no action taken. A motion was made by Nick and seconded by Bill to approve the Secretary’s Report. Vote (U) Motion passed

  4. President’s Report: Sponsors for the Fall Conference total $1,500.00 to date. Anticipated to receive an addition amount of $650.00.

    • A motion was made by Chris seconded by Gayle to have an Executive Session to discuss Personnel Issues. Vote (U) Motion passed.
      Time In: 3:15 pm Time Out: 3:42 pm No action taken as a result of the Executive Session.

    • “Blow the Whistle on Cancer Week” set by IAABO is January 18-25, 2015. It was suggested the date be the same as the week that coaches have designated as cancer awareness week.

  5. Mentoring Program: Sheridan presented the list of mentors and mentees

  6. Assignment Policy Review: Bill received feedback from the BOD on proposed changes to the assignment policy. The Assignment policy review committee will meet and review proposed changes.

  7. Interpreter’s Report: 18 currently in the prospective official’s class. Orientation for officials that passed last year was held on October 5, 2014. Only one person did not attend and that individual will not receive assignment until they meet with Dave Ames. There was discussion about the level of officials. There was discussion on possible changes. Bill expressed concern about the level system. Dave updated the BOD on the Fall Conference and reviewed the clinics for the conference. There was discussion on one official for Hampden Rec games, but no action was taken.
    A motion was made by Chris seconded by Nick to approve the Interpreter’s Report. Vote (U) Motion passed.

  8. Assignment Feedback: Randy met with the assigner and provided feedback on the meeting. Three person swap program was discussed. Discussion of pre-season tourney at Orono High School. Orono wants officials to donate game fee. No action taken

  9. A motion was made by Nick seconded by Emil to accept Joe Kennedy as a dual member. His primary Board is Board #21. Vote (U) Motion passed.
    Charlie Pray will be an inactive member for 2014-2015.

  10. New Business: Rating Form for Athletic Administrators on Arbiter. The cost to purchase the program is $99.00. A motion was made by Emil seconded by Gayle to purchase the program. Vote (U) Motion passed.

  11. Next BOD meeting will be on December 14, 2014 at 12:00 pm at the University of Maine. The meeting will be held prior to the 2:00 pm clinic on that date.

  12. President Wadleigh requested the BOD members dress with a shirt and tie for the Fall Conference (except Gayle)

  13. A motion was made by Chris seconded by Emil to adjourn the meeting at 5:14 pm. Vote (U) Motion passed
