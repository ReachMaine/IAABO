MINUTES
EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE MEETING

AUGUST 19, 2012
HAMPDEN ACADEMY
(corrected 10/29/2012)

PRESENT: Chris, Dave Ames, David Mansfield, Emil, Fred, Gayle, John, Nick, Randy, Robin, Shawn, & Sheridan
ABSENT: Wayne

  1. The meeting was called to order at 5:30 pm by President Kimball

  2. Committee Reports: Randy, Governance; Stu Marckoon, Ethics & Grievance; and Shawn, Public Relations.

  3. Short Term Goals were discussed

  4. There were no long term goals discussed

  5. Job Descriptions for Secretary and Interpreter. Committee will work on them and John & Dave will submit job descriptions to Shawn.

  6. Secretary’s Report: 136 paid members, concerns about unpaid dues & dues agreement forms from the membership. The Executive Committee directed the Secretary to send a certified copy of the letter sent to Darren Hall in March of 2012.

  7. There was a request from Andrew Weatherbee concerning his ratings for 2012-2013. He had requested to use his rating from 2011-2012 for the upcoming season. A motion was made by Randy and seconded by Chris to deny the request. Vote was 11-0-1 to deny the request. A certified letter was sent to inform the individual.

  8. Dave Ames, Interpreter updated the committee on the new official’s class, Fall Conference, clinic format, testing procedures, and meeting attendance. There will be no credit for members attending the new official’s classes. Attendance will only count as attending a clinic, but no points will be granted. Dave will email the membership this information.

  9. Dave Ames, Rating Officer updated the committee on ratings and school ratings.

  10. David Mansfield, Assigner updated the committee on the progress on scheduling for the upcoming season.

  11. The Fall 2012 Conference was discussed. It will be at Husson University on November 17th. It will include the MPA Clinic, four rules clinics, and the Fall Business Meeting. The conference will start at 8:30 am with the MPA Clinic, followed by two clinics in the morning, lunch at 12:30 pm and two afternoon clinics and concluding with the Fall Business meeting starting around 3:30 pm.

  12. New Officials class will start on September 16th at Reed Brook Middle School

  13. A motion was made by Emil and seconded by Nick to reimburse mileage at the MPA rate for travel for meetings related to the Human Rights Case. The secretary will submit the expense sheet to the President for his approval. Vote (U) Passed

  14. A motion was made by Randy and seconded by Fred to extend the meeting. Vote was (U) passed

  15. A meeting will be scheduled for mid-September to meet with George Eaton

  16. Orientation for new officials will be September 30th at 4:00 pm at Reeds Brook

  17. Dates for 2012-2013 were set with the Spring Business meeting being on March 9, 2013. The Executive Committee will meet on February 17, 2013 at 9:00 am.

  18. Secretary was to check the budget pertaining to funds for tourney mugs in 2011-2012.

  19. The meeting adjourned at 8:25 pm. A motion was made by Randy and seconded by Gayle to adjourn. Vote was (U) passed.

Respectfully submitted,

John A. Doe
Secretary
