2019 Awards Banquet
Sunday, November 17th

The Awards Banquet will be held directly following the Fall Conference on Sunday, November 17th at Jeff’s Catering in Brewer. Social at 1:30 and dinner at 2:00 pm. The Banquet will honor former Commissioner of Basketball, Mr. Peter Webb and the recipients of the Jim DiFrederico Award, Mr. Dick Durost and Mr. Harry Orser.

The DiFrederico Award is given in memory and honor of Mr. Jim DiFrederico who was a twenty-five-year member of the Eastern Maine Board of Basketball Officials before he passed away unexpectedly in 1986 at the age of 56. Mr. DiFrederico was a highly respected basketball official, outstanding citizen, gentleman, and a friend to those who crossed his path in life. He left a very favorable impression on all who had the pleasure of knowing him.
